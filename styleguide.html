Grid

The Grid serves as orientation for the size of the emoji. If possible your emojis should stay inside the Grid, but for edge cases there are 4px of padding, that can be used.

basic forms

The four basic forms define the acreage, which should be roughly the same for every icon and should always be visually balanced after finishing an icon. They don't have to be fully filled, but can be altered for your emoji.

A special case are the "Emoji Faces". They are placed inside a smaller circle to leave space for additions (such as tears or hands).

Examples

Basic Circles

The basic circles help to give a consistent look to the icon set. They should be used for rounded corners or wavy forms that can be broken down into circles.

Examples

Contour

The stroke width is 2px. The settings should be set to round corners and ends. Two overlapping contours should have a minimum gap of 2px (The line that is in the back is seperated.)

Open contour

Open contours give dynamic look. They can be used for ovelapping lines or perspective

Perspective

If possible the object should be displayed two dimensionally from the front. If it is necessary for the distinguishability you can use an orthographic projection, this only happens very rarely though.

Color

You should use the primary colors. The default is the lighter color. The darker version should be used primarily for shadows.
In exceptional cases, you can use the additional colors (orange, violet, brown and pink)
The contour is completely filled to the outer edge with color.

Topics

People

full body

people are depicted without clothing. The legs slightly run towards each other ar the ends. Armes are displayed as a single line. Static people are portrayed from the front, moving people from the side. The complexity of objects with a lot of overlapping should be reduced.

bust

The face is elliptical. There is no neck, but there is a gap between head and shoulders. The Eyes are in the middle of the head. For a neutral look, the mouth should aligned to the middle of the eyes.

Hands

The thumb runs converges slightly at the end The lower attachment of the hand is rounded. Fingers have a width of 7px.

Arrows

The arrow head is made of one basic form.

There should be a variation with one line for UI and a variation with a doubled line as an emoji

Buildings

Buildings are portrayed from the front with an entrance door. Details should be reduced.

Animals and Plants

Plants are depicted organically and open. Stems should always be just one line. Animals with big body are displayed from the side. They should be natural and the lines can be free. They should not be trivialized.

Vehicles

Emojis of vehicles should be oriented towards outlines of real vehicles to avoid a toyish look. They are simplified and depicted in a side view. The tires are placed inside the bottom line.
